# User Interviews

Three interviews conducted between 2026-05-11 and 2026-05-12 with potential users of SpendWise AI.

---

## Interview 1

**Name:** Dr Smitha Vas
**Role:**Developer 
**Company stage:** freelancer UI/UX designer
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Duration:** ~12 minutes

**Direct quotes:**
“I only realize what I spent when my bank app shows a big deduction.”
“Some tools I keep just in case, not because I use them.”
“I’d trust a tool if it doesn’t try to sell me anything.”

**Surprising insight:**

Keeps tools “just in case” → emotional retention, not usage-based.

**Design change:**

Add “unused tool detection” + usage analytics dashboard

## Interview 2

**Name:** Rohith U K
**Role:** Student CSE
**Company stage:**Final year
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Duration:** ~10 minutes

**Direct quotes:**
- “I just pay when I feel I need it, I don’t really track properly.”
“Sometimes I forget I even subscribed to tools I don’t use daily.”
“If something showed me I’m wasting money, I’d probably cancel instantly.”

**The most surprising thing they said:**
He didn’t even know exact monthly spending—very “casual payment behavior”

**What it changed about your design:**
Add a “subscription tracker + reminder” feature

## Interview 3

**Name:**Sruthy Sathyan
**Role:**Startup Intern
**Company stage:**An early stage startup
**Date:** 2026-05-12
**Duration:** ~15 minutes

**Direct quotes:**
“I honestly don’t know how much the company pays for these tools.”
“If I had visibility, I think we could cut half the tools.”
“Nobody really audits AI tools in our team.”

**The most surprising thing they said:**
No one in teams actually tracks AI tool cost properly.
**What it changed about your design:**
Add team dashboard + cost breakdown per tool
